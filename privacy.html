Farebot M: Privacy and Permissions Statement

Farebot M uses some permissions in order to allow the application to function:

  • Identity / Accounts: Used by Google Play Services.
  • Contacts / Accounts: Used by Google Play Services.
  • Internet access: Used by Google Maps to show the location of trips, and our crash reporting systems (Google Play).
  • NFC: Required to read NFC cards.
  • Photos / Media / Files: Farebot has the ability to save scanned cards to a file, and retrieve a scanned card from a file.

Farebot does not share card data with others without consent.

Farebot does not communicate with your card's issuing agency without consent.

This statement only applies to Farebot M distributed via the Google Play Store.

If in doubt, on Android 6.0 and later, you can selectively revoke permissions.
