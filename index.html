View your remaining balance, recent trips*, and other information from some contactless public transit cards using your NFC Android phone!

This application is a fork of Eric Butler's Farebot. This version supports additional cards.

* Where this information is available freely on the card.

Supported Cards

  • Bilhete Único - São Paulo, Brazil1, 2
  • Clipper - San Francisco, CA, USA
  • EZ-Link - Singapore1
  • NETS FlashPay - Singapore
  • Opal - Sydney, Australia3
  • ORCA - Seattle, WA, USA
  • OV-chipkaart - Netherlands1, 2
  • Suica, ICOCA, PASMO - Japan

1 Not compatible with all devices.

2 Requires encryption keys.

3 New in Farebot M.
